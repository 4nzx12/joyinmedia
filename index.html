Who We Are

About Joyin Media

Founded in 2024 in the heart of Kochi, Joyin Media is a full-service media production house operating out of Kaloor. We build visual narratives for brands that refuse to be ordinary — through cinematic video, editorial photography, and sharp design. No weddings. No compromises. Just precise, purposeful commercial storytelling crafted by a team that lives for the craft.
